Events!

Our main event is our fortnightly club night in which we ask that you bring your models and yourselves along as we wage war and muster our forces in The Hut, Summershill North, Cork City.

Wanting to do battle in the 8 Realms in the distant future of the 41st Millennium? We've got you covered, with many of our members being avid Warhammer: Age of Sigmar and Warhammer: 40,000 players and also fresh faced generals!

We will also be organizing several demo games ranging across various tabletop miniature games! From KillTeam, Art de le guerre, AoS, 40k, Bolt Action and more... So keep your eyes peeled and if you're interested in any of these, make sure to get in touch!

The Corkonius Sector

We have a huge Warhammer 40'000 Crusade starting soon! We have come together as a club to name our own sector within 40k space in which we can create our own lore for as we run campaigns.

We also have lots of plans in the works for events outside of club night such as Tournaments and Showcase Day! So stay tuned!

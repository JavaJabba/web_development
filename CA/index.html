About Us

The Brethren Wargaming Club Ireland is a group of like-minded individuals from across the country who enjoy waging all out miniature war on each other and those who cross their plastic strewn paths.

We are a Wargaming Group that enjoy such games as:

  • Warhammer 40,000
  • Warhammer Age of Sigmar
  • Necromunda
  • Kings of War 10mm
  • Bolt Action
  • D'Arte Le Guerre
  • Pike & Shotte
  • Silver Bayonet
  • ...And any game that takes our fancy really.

We often organize Campaigns among the group and will, with the grace of the Dice Gods, be organizing Campaigns/Tournaments in the future that will be open to those from outside our numbers. See events for more details!

Our Mantra! - Be excellent to each other!

We here at The Brethren Wargaming Club do NOT tolerate any sort of discrimination. No matter the adventure you are on, you are welcome here!

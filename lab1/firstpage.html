Education "The Journey".

I went to Mayfield Community School here in Cork City and I graduated back in 2015. Once completing the leaving cert I attended CIT studying Biological and Chemical Sciences. This did not turn out to be the path I wanted and left after year one. I then worked for a year or two while decided what I wanted to do/study. I realised that throughout my life I have always had a fascination with computers. Therefore, I started to study Computer Systems and Networks in Cork College of Commerce. I really enjoyed that course and realised that this is exactly what I wanted to do. I then attended a second year in Cork College of Commerce to study Software Development and discovered that I really like the programming aspect of computer science.

While at Cork College of Commerce, there was a new opportunity to progress into year three of a Computer Science course in the Netherlands. This seemed like an interesting opportunity so I pursed it. After applying, being accepted and moving to Amsterdam, on my first day it seemed like I was enrolled in first after previous confirmation that I was using said progression route. It turned out to be mass information given to me and my previous college about the specifics and final details of the route. Therefore I returned home to find another way of progressing on my desired path. Then salvation arrived in the form of a mature student entry to Computer Science in UCC and here I am!

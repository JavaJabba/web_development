My Favorite Hobby!

Tabletop wargaming is my favorite hobby! Tabletop wargaming is when you build, paint and play with small plastic figures in a particular setting and ruleset, be it historical such as Bolt Action a WW2 historical wargame or Warhammer: Age of Sigmar a Fantasy based setting. I am very involved in the local wargaming scene as I run Cork's biggest independent Wargaming Club called The Brethren Wargaming Club in which I am the president.
